<objective>
Add SSR (Solid-State Relay) GPIO support to the RP2040 HAL.
GP16 controls power to the BTS7960 motor driver boards — HIGH when armed, LOW otherwise.

Purpose: D-09 through D-13 — safe power-gating of BTS7960 on disarm; enables remote thermal reset.
Output: Pin constant in target.h; two new HAL functions declared in biba_hal.h, stubbed in biba_hal.c,
        and implemented with pico-sdk gpio_* calls in biba_hal_rp2040.c.
</objective>

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-02-01 | Elevation of Privilege | SSR HIGH at boot (before arming) | mitigate | biba_hal_ssr_init() drives GP16 LOW unconditionally; called from biba_hal_init() before any mode code runs |
| T-02-02 | Tampering | GP16 floating before init | mitigate | gpio_set_dir(GPIO_OUT) + gpio_put(0) in biba_hal_ssr_init; RP2040 has no internal pull on GPIO_OUT pins that override an explicit gpio_put |
| T-02-03 | Denial of Service | SSR stuck LOW (no power to motors) | accept | Fail-safe direction: stuck LOW = no motor power = safe. Failure mode requires hardware debug. |
</threat_model>
